Lionel Messi was chosen as the best footballer of 2022 by the IFFHS

Lionel Messi, captain of the Argentine national team world champion in Qatar 2022, was chosen as the best footballer of 2022 by the International Federation of Football History and Statistics (IFFHS).

The organization chose Messi as the best footballer of 2022, adding 275 points, far behind the second Mbappé with 35 and also Frenchman Karim Benzema, winner of the FIFA Ballon d'Or, with 30.

Official squad list of the Under 20 National Team for the South American

Javier Mascherano and his coaching staff announce the names of the 23 players called up to represent the Argentine National Team in the next South American Sub 20. The tournament will be held in Colombia, first at the Cali headquarters and, later, in Bogotá, from from January 19 to February 12.

Scaloni won the award for the best coach in the world from the IFFHS

The Argentine DT Lionel Scaloni won the award for the best coach of a national team in 2022 after beating the French Didier Deschamps in the vote. The International Federation of Football History and Statistics (IFFHS) reported that Scaloni won the award with 240 votes. Scaloni, DT of Argentina world champion in Qatar 2022, took a large number of votes ahead of the French Deschamps (45), who finished second ahead of the Moroccan Walid Regragui (30). Zlatko Dalic (Croatia), Hajime Moriyasu (Japan), Louis Van Gaal (Netherlands), Gregg Berhalter (United States), Tite (Brazil) and Portuguese Paulo Bento (South Korea) followed. Scaloni displaced the Italian Roberto Mancini, winner of the award in 2021.
